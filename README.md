# odendaal.uk

Personal blog and writing on AI product management, applied AI, and product leadership.

**Live at: [odendaal.uk](https://odendaal.uk/)**

## About

I'm Ruan — Head of Product at Findable, building AI document intelligence for real estate. 15 years across VC-backed startups and £1B+ enterprises, currently shipping production AI alongside engineering with Claude Code, Cursor, and a custom MCP server for my notes.

This repo holds the source for my blog. Posts focus on:

- Building with frontier AI — what works in production, what doesn't
- Product leadership and team building
- Remote work, decision-making, and how senior PMs operate
